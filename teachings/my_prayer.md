# My Prayer #
  
Oh Father God  
Who gives all,  
Son and Spirit  
Hear my call.  

Come, Lord Jesus  
By my side.  
May you always  
Be my guide.  

Holy Spirit  
In my soul,  
Grant me love and  
Make me whole.  

God, Son, Spirit,  
May you three  
Bless me now to  
Set me free.  
  
Carolyn Ruth 2010
