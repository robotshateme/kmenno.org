Normal Order of Worship (Subject to Change)

  • Welcome
  • Opening prayer and song
  • Centering Silence and confession of sin
  • Prayer and announcement of forgiveness
  • Readings & Songs of Worship / Open worship
  • Sermon/Teaching
  • Reactions
  • Joys and Concerns with Prayer
  • Announcements
  • Benediction

During the Readings we plan to follow the Revised Common Lectionary (We are in the "Year A" readings). It would be helpful to read the lectionary passages before coming. During Songs of Woship we hope that each one will bring a "psalm, a hymn or spiritual song" to share with those gathered.
