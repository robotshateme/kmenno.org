Kalamazoo Mennonite Fellowship

Kalamazoo Mennonite Fellowship is a home-based assembly affiliating with the Mennonite Church USA. We gather to listen to the Word of God together, practice a faithful life together, worship God together. We gather for worship at 10:30 to noon on Sundays, followed by a soup lunch.

Welcome!

You are invited! You are very, very welcome to join us for worship and in our life together.

A special welcome to students! If you're looking for a place to worship with a small group of loving disciples, this might be the place for you. We're very informal and you won't get lost in the crowd. We're an easy walk from Western Michigan or Kalamazoo College.
